------- INSTRUCTIONS FOR SETTING THE TIMER PLUG -----

General Information
1.	The Timer has a total of 8 ON/OFF programs.
2.	With MANUAL, ON/AUTO/MANUAL, OFF you can make the required setting easily.
3.	By clicking the 12/24-hour button you alternate between 12-hour to 24-hour mode.
4.	The Summer time function is very useful for areas using the summer time system.
5.	There are 16 combinations of days or blocks of days available for this Timer, the following (combinations are separated with |): Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri | Sat | Sun |Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun | Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri | Sat, Sun | Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat | Mon, Wed, Fr | Tue, Thu, Sat | Mon, Tue, Wed | Thu, Fri, Sat | Mon, Wed, Fri |
6.	Click the RANDOM button to make the settings execute with a random delay of 0-32 minutes. Press RANDOM again to cancel the operation.

How to set the summer time function:
1.	When in AUOT MODE, press two buttons, CLOCK and ON/AUTO/OFF, simultaneously. The LCD will show SUMMER.
2.	At the same time the clock will be set one hour back automatically.
3.	Press the two buttons again, CLOCK and ON/AUTO/OFF simultaneously, to revert to Winter time.

Initial Operation
1.	Plug the Timer into a regular 230/240 Volt household power point and turn the power on.
2.	Leave for approximately 12 hours to charge the Memory Back Up batteries.
3.	After charging, clear all current information by pressing the MASTER CLEAR button with a sharp pointed object such as a pen or pencil.
4.	The Timer is now ready to be set up for use.

Setting the Current Time
1.	Press the CLOCK button and hold, simultaneously press the WEEK button until the actual day is displayed. Continue by pressing the HOUR or the MINUTE button until the current hour or minute is displayed. During the setting, the buttons WEEK, HOUR or MINUTE can be held down for rapid forward progression.
2.	Release both buttons. The week and time are thus set.
3.	To reset incorrect time, repeat above steps.

Setting Programs
1.	Click on the TIMER button. The first ON setting can be made now.
2.	Press the WEEK button to set the day or blocks of days. Set the time by pressing the HOUR button and do the same with the MINUTE button.
3.	Press the TIMER button again to finish the first ON setting and enter into the first OFF setting. By repeating step 2 to make the first OFF setting.
4.	Press the TIMER function again to finish the first OFF setting and enter into the second ON setting. Repeat steps 2 and 3 to program the remaining settings.
5.	After completing the settings, press the CLOCK button and the Timer is ready for operation.

Example program settings:
Suppose you want to set the Timer ON at 18:15 hours and OFF at 22:15 hours. Take the following steps:
1.	Press the TIMER button. The LCD will display 1_ON.
2.	Press the WEEK button until the LCD displays “MON, TUE, WED, THU, FRI, SAT, SUN”
3.	Press the HOUR button until the LCD displays 6:00PM or 18:00
4.	Press the MINUTE button until the LCD displays 6:15PM or 18:15
5.	Press the TIMER button for the LCD to display 1_OFF.
6.	Repeat steps 3 and 4 until the LCD displays 10:15PM or 22:15.
7.	Press the RES/RCL button (the RESET / RECALL button) to reset programs, press the RES/RCL button again to recall selected programs. That button will cancel some programs or recall others.

TIP: When verifying [selected] program ensure that the settings do not overlap, especially when using the block option.

Manual ON/AUTO/MANUAL OFF setting
1.	Press the ON/AUTO/OFF button to cycle through the modes in turn.
2.	In MANUAL ON or MANUAL OFF mode, the Timer does not operate as settings in AUTO mode.
3.	When the mode is switched from MANUAL to AUTO, the Timer will keep the settings of MANUAL until the next timer setting.

12/24-Hour Mode
Press the CLOCK button simultaneously with the TIMER button. This switches from 12-hour to 24-hour mode.
